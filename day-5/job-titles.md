# Job titles to be aware of
There are a number of job titles that you can target or see as a goal after completing our training. 

Please research these and try to understand them as best as possible.

Note; You should add "Junior" the role titles below in most cases.
- DevOps Engineer
- Cloud Engineer
- Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)
- Platform Engineer
- DataOps Engineer
- Support Engineer (2nd or 3rd line)
- Automation Engineer
- Linux Systems Administrator
- Build Engineer
- Release Manager

This list is not exhaustive and keep an open mind when it comes to job title. The most important thing is what is being asked for and what the expected duties will be.
